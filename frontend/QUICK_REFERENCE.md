## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Always use real Sri Lankan companies**
   - Research on Google Maps
   - Check Facebook business pages
   - Verify phone numbers

2. **Keep descriptions scannable**
   - Short paragraphs
   - Bullet points if possible
   - Start with key info
   - End with benefit

3. **Highlight student benefits**
   - Flexibility
   - No experience needed
   - Training provided
   - Schedule around classes

4. **Use consistent formatting**
   - Same style for all descriptions
   - Standard tags format
   - Matching rate formats
   - Consistent company naming

5. **Test before publishing**
   - Check spelling
   - Verify rates are real
   - Confirm company exists
   - Proof descriptions
